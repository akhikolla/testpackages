Optimal versus heuristic cluster borders on CpG sites of a circular bacterial genome

The fast optimal circular clustering (FOCC) and the heuristic repeated \(K\)-means circular clustering (HEUC) algorithms are applied on the CpG sites of the Candidatus Carsonella ruddii genome (GenBank accession number CP019943.1).
